    A Corpus Mining System for the Coverage Ability of Models 2022

    Model’s domain effect optimization 2022

  • Based on thesis research, the interpolation method of multiple outer-LM improves the recognition effect on domain words. At present, the effects in the fields of film and TV, music and APP-stores are relatively improved by more than 30%.
  • This method has been applied to the iFLYTEK-keyboard as the highlight function of this year

    Child voice recognition optimization for speech assistant 2021

  • Refer to papers, competitions and products, the optimization scheme of using children’s data augmentation and adult voice prosody is designed.
  • Nearly7000hoursofchildren’strainingdataweregeneratedbasedontheabovemethod,whichsolvestheproblems of obtaining children’s data. The recognition rate of children’s voice is relatively increased by 32%.
  • This method has been applied to the iFLYTEK-studying-pad, and has paved the way for the company’s subsequent optimization of children’s voice effect.
